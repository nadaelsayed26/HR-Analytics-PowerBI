# HR Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## Project Overview
This project presents a **comprehensive HR Analytics Dashboard** developed using **Microsoft Power BI** to support strategic workforce analysis and informed HR decision-making.  
The dashboard transforms raw HR data into actionable insights related to employee demographics, performance, promotions, service duration, and workforce status.

---

## Business Objectives
- Provide a clear overview of workforce size and composition
- Analyze gender distribution across the organization
- Identify employees eligible for promotion
- Monitor active employees and layoffs
- Evaluate employee tenure and job levels
- Assess employee proximity to the workplace

---

## Key KPIs
- **Total Employees:** 1,470  
- **Male Employees:** 882 (60%)  
- **Female Employees:** 588 (40%)  
- **Active Employees (On-Service):** 1,353 (92%)  
- **Laid-Off Employees:** 117 (8%)  
- **Employees Due for Promotion:** 72 (5%)  
- **Employees Not Due for Promotion:** 1,398 (95%)

---

## Analytical Insights
- The workforce is predominantly active, with a low attrition percentage.
- Entry and mid-level positions (Level 1 & Level 2) represent the highest employee concentration.
- Most employees fall within short to mid-term service durations.
- A significant portion of employees live very close to the workplace, which may positively impact attendance and productivity.
- Promotion-eligible employees form a small but critical segment of the workforce.

- Interactive KPI cards
